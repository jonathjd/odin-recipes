Authentic Sicilian Pizza

This recipe was passed down by generations of residents local to the region of Sicily. For years it was kept as a guarded secret and was strictly forbidden to share this recipe with anybody who was not trained in the stove oven ways. But lately the requirements have become a little more flexible.

To do this pizza justice it is important that you adhere to the following rules.
  • Only use ingredients freshly grown in Vienna
  • The stone oven must be at least 75 years old
  • The entire pizza must be finished in one sitting.
As long as those requirements are followed, let's begin!

Ingredients

  • 1/2 Cup Flour
  • 1 tsp Yeast
  • 4 Ripe tomatoes
  • 1 Cup Sliced Bella Mushrooms
  • 3 Cups Grated Mozarella Cheese
  • 2 Garlic Cloves
  • Fresh Parsley
  • 1 Cup Sliced Pepperonies
  • Salt
  • Pepper
  • Spinach

Steps

  1. Mix flour with yeast and let rise for 36-48 hours
  2. Once dough has risen toss in air like Scicilian chef
  3. Step on ripe tomatoes with feet to make sauce (Note: WASH FEET BEFORE)
  4. Spread sauce, cheese, garlic, mushrooms, pepperonies, and parsley on top
  5. Season with salt and pepper to taste
  6. Bake pizza in stove oven for 30 minutes at 375 degrees
  7. Enjoy!
