Pizza

This recipe was passed down by generations of residents local to the region of Scicily. For years it was kept as a guarded secret and was strictly forbidden to share this recipe with anybody who was not trained in the stove oven ways. But lately the requirements have become a little more flexible.

To do this pizza justice it is important that you adhere to the following rules.
  • Only use ingredients freshly grown in Vienna
  • The stone oven must be at least 75 years old
  • The entirepizza must be finished in one sitting.
As long as those requirements are followed, let's begin!

Ingredients

  • 1/2 Cup Flour
  • 1 tsp Yeast
  • 4 Ripe tomatoes
  • 1 Cup Sliced Bella Mushrooms
  • 3 Cups Grated Mozarella Cheese
  • 2 Garlic Cloves
  • Fresh Parsley
  • 1 Cup Sliced Pepperonies
  • Salt
  • Pepper
  • Spinach
