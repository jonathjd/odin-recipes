Authentic Sicilian Spaghetti

Last but not least, we have the turn your heady spaghetti recipe that comes with meatballs and more flavor than you know what to do with!

Ingredients

  • 1 Cup Spaghetti
  • 4 Ripe tomatoes
  • 3 Cups Grated Parmesean Cheese
  • 2 Garlic Cloves
  • Fresh Parsley
  • 1 Pound Ground beef
  • 1 Pound Italian Sausage
  • Salt
  • Pepper

Steps

  1. Bring pot of water to boil
  2. Cook noodles until al dente
  3. Mix ground beef and italian sausage together and add spices
  4. Cook in cast iron skillet
  5. Step on ripe tomatoes with feet to make sauce (Note: WASH FEET BEFORE)
  6. Mix all ingredients together
  7. Season with salt and pepper to taste
  8. Enjoy!
